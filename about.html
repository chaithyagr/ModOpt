About

ModOpt was developed as part of COSMIC, a multi-disciplinary collaboration between NeuroSpin, experts in biomedical imaging, and CosmoStat, experts in astrophysical image processing. The package was designed to provide the backend optimisation algorithms for PySAP [FarrensGrigisElGueddari+20], but also serves as a stand-alone library of inverse problem solving tools. While PySAP aims to provide application-specific tools for solving complex imaging problems, ModOpt can in principle be applied to any linear inverse problem.
